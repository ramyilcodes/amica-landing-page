About Us

Amica is an accounting software that enables small scale businesses and self-employed individuals to manage their finances in other to attain success. Our mission is to help you manage your business by providing you with the tools that will hasten processes involved in accounting such as recording transactions, generating invoices, tracking income and expenses, in other to achieve success.

We started with a small team of six founders in 2023 who believe in small business owners and self-employed individuals. We believe that with Amica, bookkeeping and recording would be made easier and faster for small businesses.

As Amica, these are the values that guide us;

  • We keep it simple and easy to use even for those without an accounting background.
  • Accuracy of financial data, avoiding errors that can negatively impact your business financial health.
  • We prioritize the security of financial data, protecting it from unauthorized access, loss or theft.

Why Amica?

Works when online and offline

You can use our app without having to worry about the presence of a Wifi or cell signal. With our app, it is possible to allow any collected data, record changes, etc to be uploaded to the server when you are back online.

User-friendliness and easy navigation

Our app is easy to use and understand starting from the user interface to the features. It would provide you with an excellent user experience.

We’ve got you covered

Whether or not you have accounting knowledge, we’ve got your back. How? We built this app with simplified features to make you aware of the financial health of your business.

Simplified features just for you!

Items

Keep track of all your goods and services by recording all available and unavailable stocks. With Amica, you can also set reminder to know when your stock is running low.

Invoice

Record and write invoices anywhere and at anytime to keep track of all payments you have made. Amica makes managing invoices easy.

Profit and loss

Amica has made it easy for you to know if your business is growing or not by recording all your income and expenses on a daily basis.

Customers

Find your customers and keep track of whether or not he/she is owing you or not all in one place.
